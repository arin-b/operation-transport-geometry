# Module contracts

`otg.core.schema` contains the data model.

`otg.worlds` creates controlled operational worlds.

`otg.risks` estimates or perturbs node-wise operational risk.

`otg.invariance` builds admissible-invariance masks and penalties.

`otg.costs` constructs operational ground costs.

`otg.transport` solves balanced and unbalanced transport problems.

`otg.diagnostics` produces failure metrics and assumption reports.

`otg.reporting` writes JSON, Markdown, LaTeX snippets, and diagnostic figures.
